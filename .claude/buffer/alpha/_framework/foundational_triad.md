# Foundational Triad
**Framework group**: foundational_triad
**Framework**: true

## w:1 -- unificity
unity + multiplicity. Irreducible third modality = context = Koestler's holon = Sartre's third-party. Geometry: vortical. Structure: holarchy. Field: unific. Only unificities are actual; pure unity/multiplicity are virtual

## w:2 -- unity
modality of THINGS. Every unity is a multiplicity of at least two. Geometry: lateral. Structure: holism. Field: unitive (vision/representation)

## w:3 -- multiplicity
modality of SETS OF THINGS. Every multiplicity is a unity of at least one. Geometry: vertical. Structure: hierarchy. Field: multiplicative (ordering)

## w:4 -- [meta]
generative triad from which TAPS, RIP, and Dialectic unfold. Config space = 1, each modality is a 1-prime (different mode of being-one)
