## Integration Points

- **SixCriteria_3plus3=LocalVsEmergent**: The optimization/maximization split is the most architecturally significant contribution from this source. Criteria 1-3 (elbow room, learning, variety) are properties of individual agents — measurable from the per-agent L-matrix ledger (L11 proportion, type_set growth rate dK/dt, type_set diversity). Criteria 4-6 (mutual support, meaningfulness, desirable future) are emergent GROUP properties requiring specific network topology — mutual support IS reciprocal L12/L21 links (Lizier's w^cyc_{i,2}), meaningfulness requires feedforward paths connecting individual action to group outcome. This means DP2 is not merely preferable but STRUCTURALLY NECESSARY for the maximization criteria — a testable simulation prediction.

- **DP_exhaustiveness=BinaryAttractor**: The website's claim that DP1 and DP2 are "the only two" design principles constrains the simulation's topology space. Every coordination relationship in family groups must resolve to one of two attractors: hierarchical (one agent's L21 dominates others) or self-managing (mutual L12/L21 between peers). There is no stable third state. Laissez-faire is not a third attractor but the unstable transient where the system has not yet settled. This maps to the dynamical systems concept of a saddle point — the system passes through laissez-faire but cannot remain there.

- **LaissezFaire=DemetatheticVoid**: The emphatic distinction between laissez-faire and DP2 maps to a critical modeling concern. Laissez-faire has L12/L21 channels that carry no coherent signal — agents interact but without metathetic structure. In sigma-TAP: low clustering coefficient, low information storage, no shared TAPS signature. This is the user's "disintegration" mechanism (§5.3) — temporal state 0 for groups. Agents exist but the group has no organizing principle. The simulation must be able to represent this state distinct from both DP1 and DP2.

- **TwoLayer_environment=FieldArchitecture**: The SC methodology's explicit two-layer model (wider L22-dominant + immediate L12/L21-dominant) maps to the user's four-field architecture (§5.56): the global/nonlocal field (L11+L22) carries value-structure (wider environment), while the assembly field (L12+L21) carries operational coordination (immediate environment). The SC process moves from global field → assembly field; the PDW then restructures the assembly field. This provides methodological grounding for the four-field implementation sequence.

- **L22_incommensurability=AutonomousDrift**: The footwear manufacturer case demonstrates L22 incommensurability empirically: perfect internal adaptation (DP1→DP2) was insufficient because the environment's causal texture (supplier networks, logistics chains) operated under different laws and could not co-adapt at the same rate. For sigma-TAP: the environmental drift mechanism must be genuinely autochthonous — self-amplifying environmental changes that create irreversible reorganization independent of agent actions. Currently a 1% random shift; this case suggests it should be capable of cascade dynamics.

- **Neoliberalism=CounterFinality**: Merrelyn Emery's account of neoliberalism arresting the DP2 movement maps to Sartre's counter-finality — the practico-inert (sedimented market ideology, managerial prerogative) reversing active praxis (the global DP2 movement). DP2 organizations, by succeeding within the capitalist frame, may have contributed to the very conditions neoliberalism weaponized. This is the "progress as changing to remain the same" pattern — relevant to modeling how environmental counter-forces (L22 shifts) can reverse group-level organizational gains (L11 transformation).

- **JointOptimization=SigmaFunction**: The website's "holy grail" formulation confirms sigma(Xi) as the joint optimization function. sigma is NOT merely a growth rate multiplier but the mechanism through which social structure (interaction history, trust, topology) modulates technical performance (innovation rate, type_set growth). Neither can be optimized independently. This extends the existing w:91 mapping (joint optimization from Emery_M_CurrentVersionOST_2000_Paper) with the practitioner-level emphasis on why isolated optimization fails.

- **AboriginalDP2=EmpiricalOptimum**: The claim that Aboriginal and Islander cultures operated DP2 structures for cooperative culture across a continent positions DP2 not as modern normative preference but as empirically-observed structural optimum for cooperative systems persisting in complex environments over deep time. For sigma-TAP: validates that the DP2 attractor should be robust across diverse parameter settings — the simulation should show DP2 emerging as favored under cooperation-demanding conditions regardless of specific initial configuration.

## Open Questions

1. **Six Criteria measurability**: The mapping from Six Criteria to sigma-TAP observables (L11 proportion for elbow room, dK/dt for learning, etc.) is plausible but not validated. The optimization/maximization distinction implies fundamentally different measurement types — local metrics vs emergent group properties. Should the simulation implement Six Criteria as a diagnostic layer, or are they implicit in existing metrics?

2. **Laissez-faire dynamics**: The website insists laissez-faire is categorically distinct from DP2. In sigma-TAP, what distinguishes a DP2 group with very low coordination from laissez-faire? Is it the presence/absence of shared goals (TAPS alignment), or the structural motif (reciprocal loops vs disconnected channels)?

3. **DP exhaustiveness under simulation dynamics**: The "only two" claim is about genotypical structure, not phenotypical variation. In a continuous simulation, how does the binary genotype (DP1/DP2) manifest? Is there a sharp phase transition, or do groups exist on a continuous spectrum with DP1 and DP2 as basin attractors?

4. **Aboriginal knowledge validation**: Merrelyn Emery cites Aboriginal/Islander DP2 structures as empirical evidence. The user's framework already draws on deep historical/philosophical traditions. Does this claim require explicit engagement with Indigenous systems theory, or is it sufficient as supporting evidence for DP2's universality?
